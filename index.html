Aim For The Eutopia above !

From the downfall of Babylon to marching armies, the pen has seen it all which feels like a distant memory in today’s fast paced world. But if you have the fire, if you have the hunger, if you have what it takes to get literature back to its lost place, UEM Kolkata has got you covered. Name your talent and we are here to provide you with the stage. Make an act, and subject the world to change. Presenting to you, our very own Literary fest, Eutopia’23, organized by UEM, Kolkata on the 14th and 15th January in the college premises to keep this eternal flame of literature burning in the young minds and hearts.
