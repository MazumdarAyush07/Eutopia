Rules

    This will be a solo event(schools and colleges inclusive). Each participant will be given a time of maximum 3 mins (+/- 20 secs) to showcase their verse. The participants will be judged accordingly by the judges on the basis of these areas:

  • Originality
  • Flow
  • Structure
  • Modularity
  • Lyricism (no swearing)
